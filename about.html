About Me

I'm a Mathematics and Computer Science student at NYU with a deep fascination for the intersection of quantitative finance, artificial intelligence, and elegant system design.

My journey has taken me from the trading floors of New York to the bond desks of China, where I've developed AI-powered trading tools and market simulation systems. I'm passionate about creating technology that can understand and predict complex market behaviors.

Beyond the world of algorithms and data, I find inspiration in jazz music, the timeless prose of Jane Austen, and contemporary Japanese literature. This diverse range of interests often influences my approach to problem-solving and creativity in my technical work.

I believe in the power of preparation meeting opportunity, and I'm always eager to explore new frontiers in technology and finance.
