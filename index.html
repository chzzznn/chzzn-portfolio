About Me
In the fields of observation, chance favors only the prepared mind -- Louis Pasteur
Hi, I’m Nicole Zhang.

I'm currently studying Mathematics and Computer Science at NYU, with a passion for algorithmic trading, AI research, and elegant systems design.

I’ve built AI-powered trading tools and supported bond desks in both New York and China. I craft simulations of markets, forecast storms with multimodal Machine Learning, and dive into jazz, Jane Austen, and Japanese lit. My projects span from deep learning weather models to game-based economic engines blending macro models with blockchain mechanics.

When I’m not coding or researching, you’ll find me getting lost in museums and mysteries.

Always learning, always building. and always curious about what lies beyond the next dataset or dice roll.
